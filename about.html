About Delta Parker Productions

Delta Parker Productions is an independent game studio focused on creating high-quality RTS (real-time strategy) gameplay experiences and experimental zombie mechanics. This site is a preview space for assets, prototypes, and development progress — a place to show what I'm building as I go.

A few important facts about the studio and vision:

  • Lifetime gamer & solo dev: I'm a lifetime gamer building games because I love making them. I'm just one person with a laptop — no studio funding right now, just head-down, all-in effort.
  • Focus: My primary focus is RTS gameplay — balanced systems, AI-driven hordes, and fun unit design at medium → high quality targets.
  • What you'll see here: Early asset previews, design notes, development updates, and experimental systems I test while building prototypes.
  • Funding: I have no external funding at the moment — community support (donations) helps a lot and lets me keep iterating.

If you like what you see, check the Dev Blog for frequent updates and the Games page for demos and downloads. Thanks for stopping by — every view and share helps.

— Delta Parker, 2025
