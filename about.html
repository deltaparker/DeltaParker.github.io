About My Game Development Journey

I'm a lifetime gamer diving head-first into game development with nothing but a laptop, passion, and a vision. No funding, no team — just determination, creativity, and the will to build something awesome from nothing.

My focus is on RTS gameplay with gritty, apocalyptic themes and medium to high-quality visuals. This page showcases my progress, asset previews, and the evolution of my skills as I push myself to build something truly unique.

Everything here is built from scratch. Every line of code, every unit, every visual — crafted as I learn, grow, and fight to bring this world to life.
